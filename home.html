CBC Test

A Complete Blood Count (CBC) test is a common blood test that evaluates overall
health and detects a wide range of conditions,
including infections, anemia, and immune system disorders.

Thyroid Function
Tests (TFTs)

TFTs assess thyroid health by measuring TSH, T3, and T4 levels.
These tests help diagnose thyroid disorders like hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism.
Visit our lab for accurate thyroid testing.

Blood Glucose Test

A Blood Glucose Test measures sugar levels in the blood to diagnose and monitor diabetes and prediabetes.
It helps assess how well the body processes glucose. Visit our lab for reliable blood sugar testing.

Urinalysis

A Urinalysis is a routine test that examines urine to detect infections, kidney disease, diabetes, and other health conditions. It checks for abnormalities in color, clarity, and composition. Visit our lab for accurate urine testing.
